Calling convention:

- AMD64:
	- Arguments: rdi, rsi, rdx, rcx, r8, r9
	- Callee-saved: rbx, rsp, rbp, r12-r15
- AArch64:
	- Arguments: r0-r7
	- Callee-saved: r19-r29, sp, v8-v15
- LoongArch64:
	- Arguments: a0-a7
	- Callee-saved: s0-s9, fs0-fs7
